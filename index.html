About Me

I am Daniel Awotundun, enrolled at the University of Ottawa. I am interested in human capital multipliers. Individuals are able to iterate quickly using the power of computers to make things like Stardew Valley, and there is a new term for it called "Solopreneur." If there is a problem, I prefer to roll up my sleeves and start breaking things. My motto is: [Fail Fast, Fail Forward]. I also love working with teams but every Championship team must consist of Championship Players. I believe that my endeavors and skills can be valuable to a plethora of projects.

Some example of human capital multipliers are:

  • Web Development - An idea or a business just needs a link for a global customer base.
  • ChatGPT and other LLMs - Information, skill acquisition, debugging tool, etc.
  • MidJourney/DALL-E - Non-artists are able to communicate ideas using art to tell stories.
  • Youtube/Twitter - Information dissemination and community building.

I think the intersection of all these tools and various APIs makes a powerful and well equipped human in this information age. The possibilities are endless. As a result, I have decided to dedicate my time and effort to information dissemination mediums.
